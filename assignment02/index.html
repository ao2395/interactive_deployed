Assignment 2: Breakout!

Tutorial:
1. Left click to begin playing.
2. A and D to move the paddle left and right.
3. Keep the ball in play!
4. Each Treasure grants point(s)
5. The ball going out of play results in losing all your points.
6. Left click again to restart the game after a loss.

Technical Specifications:
1. Each time the ball bounces off a border, its speed increases.
2. Hitting the paddle near the edges produces 'softer' rebounds.
3. Hitting the paddle from the middle produces 'sharper' rebounds.
4. Points are based on the ball speed, up to 3 points.
