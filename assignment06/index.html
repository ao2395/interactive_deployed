Game Rules

Tetris AR Rules

  • Objective: Complete horizontal lines to score points and avoid reaching the top of the grid.
  • Controls: Use Your hand to move blocks left and right. Try to keep your hand on screen and a good distance away from the camera
  • Scoring: You earn 10 points for each completed line.
  • Game Over: The game ends when blocks stack up to the top of the grid.
  • Tip: Play on a Laptop! Move the laptop further away than normal for better tracking. You also might be tempted to move your hand forward while playing but try to keep the movement to a 2d plane!
  • Warning: This game is not optimized for a mobile phone screen, please play on a laptop
